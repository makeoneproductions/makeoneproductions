About Make One Productions

Make One Productions is a cinematic video production company based in Edmonton, Alberta. Specializing in wedding films and commercial video content, we bring a refined, story-driven approach to every project.

Founded by Aiden Brost, Make One Productions was created to capture moments that matter — whether it’s the intimate emotion of a wedding day or the energy and branding of a business. We believe in the power of visual storytelling to move people, build brands, and preserve memories.

With a background in both film and music production, our style is melodic, and emotionally immersive. From concept to final cut, we work closely with clients to deliver visually stunning and emotionally powerful films.

Let us help you tell your story — cinematically.
